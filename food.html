Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Edamame
  • Corn
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Garlic

  1. Chop the carrots into thin slices, peel the edemame, and mince the garlic.
  2. Place a pan on the stove and turn to medium
  3. Put the corn, carrots, and edamame in the pan with some butter and cook
  4. Add in the rice and garlic and stir
  5. Drizzle in soy sauce and add more butter and stir
  6. Enjoy!
