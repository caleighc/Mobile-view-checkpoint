My College Life



I am a student at the University of Michigan I am studying computer science. I am applying to the School of Information where I want to pursue a UX Design major. I am originally from Los Angeles, California. At home I live 5 minutes from the beach so I love to surf and swim on the weekends. Coming from California all the way to Michigan has been a big adjustment for me so I am going to share some of my college tips!

Above is a photo I took right after I got accepted to the University of Michigan!

It is very important to have a balanced lifestyle in college. School can be very challenging so it is very important to have outlets to release stress. I rely a lot on my friends to keep me sane but I also meditate to destress. It is very important to have a consistent meal plan. Last, it is crucial to have well thought out schedule to get all your schoolwork done.
